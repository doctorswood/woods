About Doctor's Wood

Beautiful semi-ancient woodland in Kent. Situated ten minutes drive from Faversham. Home to a variety of wildlife, trees and plants. Lovingly managed as a site of conservation, we host a wide variety of workshops and activities that support and help groups, individuals, families, friends and communities to reconnect, encourage creativity through the engagement with arts & nature. This includes art and music workshops & woodland based craft activities.

Thank you!

Our mixed woodland extends over 7 acres and is home to hazel, ash, chestnut, oak, silver birch, hornbeam, alder, yew wild orchids, woodland anemones, dogs mercury, bluebells, mullein, lords and ladies, celandine among others. So far we've spotted buzzards, dormice, squirrels, rabbits, a badgers set & woodpeckers. Since meeting, Rick and I shared a common passion: trees, woodlands, bonfires, art, music and community. We both believe in the power of reconnecting with nature through a creative activity and with one another can have on the human spirit . We know how healing, even just a few hours spent contributing to the conservation, appreciation and sustainable engagement with the natural environment can be and we believe people need this connection now more than ever. So when the opportunity and funding to purchase Our Healing Woodland came about, we were just too excited to let the opportunity go by and we simply can't not share it . We have many interesting creatives and woodland minded people we have to invited to our place and we have asked them to run workshops to inspire, teach and educate.
